MY FAVORITE EVENTS IN BUKSU

NSTP CWTS 1st BATCH TREE PLANTING

October 18 2023

Luckily, we are the first batch of CWTS first-year students participating in the event. We had lots of fun times and great memories, but if we want to have windy, cold air and less humidity, we need to grow trees. That was one of my favorite things to do in college.

First Welcoming Meeting of BSIT and BSEMC

August 19 2023

Since it was the first welcoming meeting of BSIT and BSEMC, I was ecstatic and nervous about the event. The instructors' talks were inspiring, and the program was enjoyable and fun. I also made a lot of friends that day, and I'm thankful that they are still my friends now.

First General Assembly of College of Technologies

November 19

One of the most significant events for an IT student is this assembly, which discusses current affairs and future goals for the computer society. All COMSOC members make sure the event is exciting and dynamic, and their suggested plans for the future are presented clearly.
